Research @ the Computer-Human Interaction Lab (CHIL)

I currently work as an undergraduate research assitant under Dr. Michael Byrne of the Computer Human Interaction Laboratory at Rice University. My current project involves learning ACT-R (a cognitive architecture and Python package) used for modeling in order to create simulations based off of eyetracking data collected from paper ballot voting. By creating these models, the lab hopes to create simulations to analyze and score the usability of such systems.
